They are not used as sources for exploit walkthroughs or challenge solutions.

- Responsible disclosure principles  
  Emphasize minimizing harm by avoiding unnecessary disclosure of sensitive

## Disclaimer

All references listed above are publicly available educational or professional
resources.

This repository intentionally avoids linking to:
- Exploit databases
- Challenge walkthroughs
- Command-level tutorials
- Credential or payload disclosures

The intent is to demonstrate security understanding and reporting maturity,
not to facilitate misuse or shortcut learning.
